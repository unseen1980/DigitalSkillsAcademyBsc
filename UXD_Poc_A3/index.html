Ethical Eaters Mission

What is Ethical Eating?

Ethical Eaters is all about empowering you with the knowledge to reduce your Carbon and Water Footprints by making informed decisions about the food you eat. Use our Food Tracker to see how much carbon is emitted.

What is Carbon Footprint?

A Carbon Footprint is the amount of carbon released as a gas into the atmosphere. It can be calculated for a product, service, person or even a country, and is used to understand the impact human activity is having on.

Reduce my Carbon Footprint?

The simplest way to reduce your Carbon Footprint would be to minimize your food wastage from the cooking habits. By being careful about the amount of food you cook and eat you can easily reduce food wastage.

What is a Water Footprint?

Water is the single most essential component for life. A Water Footprint is the amount of freshwater used by individuals, groups or companies in order to make goods or provide services.

Where does Ethical Eaters get their information?

Ethical Eaters source data from a wide range of reputable and reliable and peer reviewed sources such as government agencies, the United Nations, World Health Organisation and respected NGO’s.

How does the Food Tracker work?

The Food Tracker is a way for you to select the food you eat and see how much carbon is emitted into the atmosphere and how much water is used during production.

Reduce water Footprint?

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle. The more food we consume the more water is used during its production.

Low Carbon Footprint Food?

Some of the less carbon-rich foods include lentils, cereals, beans and nuts since their carbon footprint is lower than protein-based foods.

About us
